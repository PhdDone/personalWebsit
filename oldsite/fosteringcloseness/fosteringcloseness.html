Design Challenge

With more and more people using social network to communicate, the problem of social noise is becoming more and more obvious. We don't have private space to share the information with the people we care, we have hundreds of "friends" in the social network but the meaning of "friend" is changing at the same time. How to foster closeness? How to avoid social noise without breaking away from technology?


Design Process

We began with restating the design problem and defining the target people as strong ties with long distance.

 

During our design research, we performed a user survey about the current situations of how people communicate with their strong ties, like families, closed friends and partners. We found that people prefer to use calls, video calls, short messages to contact their strong ties because it's natural, private and efficient. We also found that people prefer images communications like videos or photos to share with their strong ties, through which they can share the experience and life.

 

Our idea is an integrated photo sharing application on mobile phone, in which people can call, video call and share photos with their strong ties through internet. That is a combination of the characters of social network and real time communication tools. We also think about the one bit short message, through which you can just click a button to send people you care the missing message.

 

Our whole design process and the final design are in the slide show above.


Reflection

This project is to practice our abilities of expansion and contraction. We did the research before we have a clear view of who we designed for and what the context we were going for. But what we should do is defining the target people and design context first and then do the related design research. Another lesson we learnt from this project is design is about details, it's better to do one concept well rather than dealing with many concepts at the same time.
