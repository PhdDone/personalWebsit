Type: Interaction Design | Time: February 2012

Tool Set: Illustrator, Powerpoint, Photoshop

Design Methods: Ideation, Scenario, Prototyping

PDF Format: Pillow Messager

Design Challenge

Current SMS through COLD touchscreen: we can’t see the express, we can’t feel the emotions, we can feel the boundary clearly between ourselves and the people at another end, we can only see the texts which sometimes cheat on us. The only thing we can touch and feel is: this piece of screen!


Design Rational

This SMS pillow will translate the wireless codes into voice, so you can hear human sound! This SMS pillow can also give out vibration, heat when you hug it and other actions simulated from the sender. When you want to send a message, just talk to this pillow. When you feel angry, you can shake it, throw it or even beat it! Of course, if you want to kiss the receiver, just hug the pillow and kiss it. You will hear the message instead of texts. You can hear the pillow scream because of the beat it bear… you know, the sender is angry with you. And, you can hug it to feel the heat and hear the kiss!


Communication needs not only texts, but more about touch, sound and other senses. Without operation through the screen, maybe you will receive more natural and safe feeling.
