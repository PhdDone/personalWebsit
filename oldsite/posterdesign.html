Undergraduate Inventory

Type: Graphic Design | Time: October 2012

Tool Set: Sketch, Photography, Photoshop, InDesign

PDF Format: Bills of Rights Amedment Four

Overview

This is a poster design for the Bills of Rights Amendment Four. The content of this amendment is: "Search and arrest: the right of the people to be secure in their persons, houses, papers, and effects, against unreasonable searches and seizures, shall not be violated, and no Warrants shall issue, but upon probable cause, supported by Oath or affirmation, and particularly describing the place to be searched, and the persons or things to be seized." I used a photo of door lock and chain to represent the meaning of "secure".
