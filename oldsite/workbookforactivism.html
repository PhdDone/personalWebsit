PDF Format: Workbook for Activism

Design Challenge

 

For this workbook, we chose to focus on the failures of moving from activism to action, seeking to understand procedural breakdown and its causes.


Design Process

Through the activist events we have observed and the interviews we have done with activism professionals and activists, we found there are three different groups of participants: activist, observer, and supporter. And we also came up with three assumptions about the causes for the procedure breakdown: 1. Cause (or problem space) needs to stay current; 2. Single vs Group and 3. Information disconnect.


We have five insights from our research evidence and we totally came up with 25 design concepts. The insights we obtained are:


1. HUMAN-CENTERED ACTIVISM

2. INFORMATION DISCONNECT

3. RETHINKING ACTIVISM AS SOCIAL INNOVATION

4. START LOCAL. SCALE GLOBAL.

5. ACTIVISM AS SOCIAL CURRENCY


For every insight, we came up with about five different design concepts.
