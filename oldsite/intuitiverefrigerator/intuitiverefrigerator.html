Type: Interaction Design | Time: February 2012

Tool Set: Illustrator, Powerpoint, Photoshop

Design Method: Ideation, Scenario, Prototyping

PDF Format: Intuitive Refrigerator

Design Challenge

To interact with a refrigerator is something we all do several times every day. The interaction is simple and may not really involve any memorable experiences. The main purpose is to make the refrigerator more interactive and different. The challenge is to design an interaction with a refrigerator that is not part of the refrigerator itself, that is, it cannot be a screen on the refrigerator. This means that the interaction/interface has to be something that is away from the refrigerator.


Design Rational

Add food scanner and food sensors within the current refrigerator. The scanner can scan all the bar-code outside the food; the sensor can scan the food itself. All the information will together generate a statistical result. The intuitive wall can show the food consumption and will recommend food to users according to their personal recordings.


Refrigerator working with the interactive wall can give a clear view of the nutrition composition. People may have a better understanding of food in the refrigerator. People can choose different food plans, by which they can keep watch on their eating habits. People can also gain food recommendations, in which way the refrigerator works as the nutrition expert.
